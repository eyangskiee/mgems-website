Who We Are

About MGEMS

Brgy. Manuel Guianga Emergency Medical Services is a community-driven emergency response team dedicated to providing quality pre-hospital care to the residents of Barangay Manuel Guianga.

Composed of dedicated volunteers and professionals — from first responders and EMTs to registered nurses and emergency medicine physicians — MGEMS stands ready to serve at any hour. Our mission is rooted in compassion, speed, and excellence in emergency care.

24/7 Response
EMT Certified Staff
BLS Trained Team

Mission

To provide prompt, professional, and compassionate emergency medical care to every resident of Barangay Manuel Guianga.

Vision

A barangay where every resident has access to world-class pre-hospital emergency medical services.
